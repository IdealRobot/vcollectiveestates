How It Works

A simple, structured process designed to maximize value and minimize stress.

1. Consultation

We walk the property with you, understand your goals, and evaluate the contents to determine the best approach.

2. Sort & Organize

Items are carefully sorted, categorized, and prepared for sale or consignment.

3. Price & Stage

We research, price, and professionally stage items to create a clean, appealing shopping experience.

4. Sale Event

We manage the sale across the appropriate channels — in-person, online, or both — to reach the right buyers.

5. Payout

After the sale, you receive a clear breakdown and prompt payment, with options for cleanout if needed.
